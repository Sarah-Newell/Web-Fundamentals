Grooming

Professional Grooming

Our professional Groomers have years of experience and love working with dogs. K9 Kampus has a beautiful spacious grooming facility that is also cage free! Our daycare patrons that have been temperament tested are allowed to walk around freely in the grooming facility while they wait their turn. We provide a loving and caring environment to alleviate any anxiety while they are in our grooming facility.

Please note that to use our grooming services you must show proof of vaccinations to enter the facility. Rabies, Bordetella, Canine Influenza and DHLPP are required.

Call to get detailed information and schedule a grooming appointment at 321-622-8971.

Boarding

All of our Luxury suites have personal webcams, allowing only the registered pet parent to view your dog in your suite. All our themed suites have flat screen TV's, pet potty's and night lights. Out Basic suites have Kuranda beds and our themed suites have either a Hammock bed or a specialty bed.

15x13 Family Suite: The only suite in Brevard that can accommodate dogs, cats, birds, etc from the same family in one suite. Pricing varies depending on types of animals and number of animals.

Daycare

Daycare is Available:

Monday thru Friday between 7am and 5pm, Saturday & Sunday 9am-4pm (Reserved Daycare dogs must be dropped off before 11:00am, walk-in/drop-in daycare dogs must be in the facility by 10am) Sunday is by reservation only. Monday thru Friday we provide a 45 minute pickup grace period for pick ups, after 5:45pm additional fees will apply. Please note that late pick ups need to be pre-arranged.

Daycare Reservations are required for daycare. Clients are to reserve their reservations via their online client portal for daycare no -later than 5pm the evening before (Tuesday thru Saturday) and 4pm the evening before (Saturday and Sunday)

Daycare fee is $35/day, we provide a $5 discount ($30/day) if you are reserved by end of daycare the day before you want to attend. Reserving in advance will also qualify you for any specials we have for that day (i.e. Free Pool Party). If you are NOT reserved discounts for daycare cannot be applied, which includes pre-paid daycare packages.

We will still accept call-ins/walk-ins however, daycare is at a higher rate and does not qualify for any current specials being offered. If we reach capacity we may have to turn away non-reserved clients. You can reserve in person with the front desk, call in to the front desk, or reserve using our online system via your client login. We do not recommend email as we are not always able to check email regularly. Walk-ins & Call-ins must be in the facility no later than 10:00am.

We do recommend that you verify your daycare reservations by logging into your client file. The weblink is https://secure.petexec.net/login.php your username should be your email address and your password is your zipcode. If you have any issues with your client file please contact us.

Excursions (extra services): You can add extra fun time to your dogs stay by including excursions to your dogs dogcation. We offer Pool Play, Lure Chasing, Private Play time, etc.
