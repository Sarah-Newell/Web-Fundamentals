Daycare Rules and Regulations
  • Daycare ends at 5pm but we realize many parents are just getting out of work at 5pm so we give you a grace period until 5:45pm to pick up your kids. Please know that promptly after 5:45 we start charging $5.00/hour/dog for pick-up until our closing hour and $20/hour/dog after closing hour. We are very strict with our schedules.
  • There are no graces periods for weekend daycare pick-ups. Therefore after 4pm there is a $20/hour/dog fee.
  • If you have any questions about our hours of operation, and pricing procedures please see our FAQ's.
Early Drop-Offs:
  • K9 Kampus understands that there will be times that our clients may need to drop off before outdoors are officially open.
  • Accommodation can be made for early drop-offs by REQUEST only. Early drop-offs must be pre-arranged and a $20/hour per dog prior to opening fee must be pre-paid. This is a non-refundable fee.
  • It is important to understand that all our attention is given to our current boarders prior to opening. When a dog enters the facility for daycare or boarding drop-off it requires additional staff to be on-site, therefore, we require pre-arrangements and fees for early drop-offs to be pre-paid.
  • Please note that if you arrive before our hours of operation, you may not be allowed entry until we are officially open.
  • If you are allowed entry into the facility before official hours, you will be charged $20/per hour that you are early.
Bording Rules and Regulations

Please note that using the online boarding reservation system is a REQUEST for boarding only. Your request is confirmed only when a 50% deposit has been received. If you are a current client with a credit card on file, by completing the online Boarding Reservation Request form you KNOWINGLY allow K9 Kampus to automatically process your card on file to finalize your Boarding Reservation Request. If you DO NOT want us to use the credit card you have on file DO NOT use the online Boarding Request Form. Please be sure the credit card information we have on file is accurate.

If you DO NOT have a credit card on file with us your Boarding Reservation Request is NOT confirmed until we receive a 50% deposit. Please call us with this information at your earliest convenience.K9 Kampus requires a credit card on file to participate in boarding.

If you are a new client please know that Boarding reservations can't be made until a Temperament Evaluation has been completed. Please contact K9 Kampus at 321-622-8971 to schedule. Temperament Evaluations must be pre-scheduled and pre-paid and can't be reserved on-line. The temperament evaluation fee is non-refundable.

General Information:

Boarding Drop-Off Times: 8:30am-11am Monday-Friday and 9:30am-11am Saturday & Sunday.

Arriving after 11am up to 1pm is an additional fee of $5/hr/dog. After 1pm there is a $25 fee/dog and you may be turned away for arriving after the required time. It is recommended and preferred to have all boarders dropped off no later than 11am so that they can get a lot of attention and play time so they are nice and tired for bed. This is especially important for those dogs that experience night-time anxiety while away from home.

Pick-Up Times: 8:30am-3pm Monday-Friday and 9:30am-3pm Saturday & Sunday. We appreciate it when boarding dogs are picked up prior to our busy times of the day.

Any deviations to drop-offs and pick-up must be pre-arranged with K9 Kampus and may be subject to additional fees.

Boarding Fees:

Our boarding fees are calculated based on a 24 hour period. For example if you dropped off your dog at 9am Monday and picked up your dog at 9am Tuesday your fee would be the one night boarding fee with no additional charges (unless you added excursions onto your dogs stay). If you drop off at 9am Monday but do not pick up until 5pm on Tuesday you will be charged the one night boarding fee and one day of daycare. Unlike other facilities we do not automatically charge you another night of boarding if you don't pick up by a certain time, we only charge you for how long you are in the facility after your drop in time.

HOLIDAY Boarding:

Certain holidays require a holiday fee per day. Other facilities will increase their fees for specific seasons, this increase all services for all clients during their specified season. K9 Kampus does not have increased fees for all services during specified seasons. We only charge holiday fees for the clients that are using our services during the actual holidays. Our fees are usually $10/dog/day for the designated season and $5/cat or small animal/day/ (Holidays fees are subject to change and my vary from holiday to holiday. Please be sure to confirm the fees for any holiday you are planning boarding for. Holiday fees are do at the time of making your reservation and are Non-Refundable, no exceptions.

On most holidays K9 Kampus does NOT offer any drop-offs or pick-ups. If exceptions are made by the owner an additional drop off fee will apply at a minimum of $50/dog/cat/small animal.

IMPORTANT- Boarding Information/Checklist:
  • Bring food, in pre-packaged serving sizes. (Please do not bring large containers)
  • To eliminate any confusion or mistakes K9 Kampus requires that you portion out each feeding in clearly marked individual bags.
  • Because we have had issues with receiving enough food for board stays, if you do not pre-portion out the food,K9 Kampus will do it for you and you will be charged a $10/week fee.
  • If you don't provide enough food for your dogs stay, K9 Kampus will use our house food and you will be charged a $2/meal charge.
  • K9 Kampus staff will do our best to follow your special feeding instructions.
  • Please DO NOT bring large amounts of excess food or large containers.
  • Please DO NOT bring bowls.
  • Bedding/blankets or any items that will make your pet more comfortable while he is staying with us. (Please mark each item with your pets name)
  • You are welcome to bring personal items from home, but you do so at your own risk. K9 Kampus is not responsible for the condition of your personal items. We wouldn't want any of your items to get lost in transit or ruined and therefore recommend that you do not bring anything of value or anything sentimental.
  • K9 Kampus does NOT launder soiled personal items.
  • Toys: are allowed in their personal suite.
  • Treats: If you bring treats they will be given while in their suite.
  • Please provide detailed directions for feedings and medication.
  • Please provide local emergency contact information.
  • A boarding agreement will need to be signed when you arrive if you haven't done so already. If you are boarding for the first time please go to our website and read our boarding agreement thoroughly as it explains our cancellation policy, fee structure and other details required for your boarding reservation. A boarding agreement needs to be signed for each boarding reservations.
  • Medications: Please note that K9 Kampus Pet Pals will administer medications at your request, however, we are NOT Veterinarians or Veterinarian technicians, therefore we are not lisenced or trained in administering medications and we rely on the instructions you provide us.
    • K9 Kampus recommends that you board your pet with a Veterinarian if your furry family member requires medications that are vital to your pets health and well being.
    • When you arrive at K9 Kampus it is important to inform us upon entering the facility that you need medications administered.
    • Please only bring the number of tablets/servings needed for their stay in their original packaging/bottle.
    • You will be required to sign a Medication Administration Disclosure/Waiver stating that you assume all risks associated with having K9 Kampus administer medications to your pet and you hold harmless K9 Kampus and it's employees for any and all issues that may arise in regards to your request to administer medications to your pet.
    Cancellation Policy:
