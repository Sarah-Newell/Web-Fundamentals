Vaccinations are Required

It is the owner's responsibility to provide proof of vaccinations for Rabies, Distemper/Parvo, Bordetella, and Canine Influenza. We only accept vaccinations on Veterinarian Letterhead. All vaccinations must be veterinarian administered. We do not accept breeder or self-administered vaccinations. Currently, we do not accept titers.
